Event description
Women Cyclists: Are you riding through common bike discomfort or pain? What is "reasonable" vs "unnecessary pain" when riding your bike? Did you know that a majority of common cycling related ailments can be alleviated by simple bike fit and conditioning concepts? Join us online as Kari Studley, PT, DPT presents strategies to prevent and minimize common cycling discomforts and optimize your training for RAMROD, STP or other amazing endurance cycling goals.

More on Kari Studley
Kari was the first woman to cross the RAMROD finish line in 2003. Kari has over 10 years of elite and professional level race experience in mountain bike, cyclocross, and road cycling. She is a UCI Masters World Cyclocross Champion, three-time National Cyclocross Champion, 2009 Single Speed Cyclocross World Champion and 6 time Washington State Cyclocross Champion. When not on the bike, Kari merges her experience and passion for cycling and human kinetics as a bike fit specialist at Corpore Sano. Kari received her Bachelor of Science in Sport and Exercise Science from Western Washington University, and her Doctorate of Physical Therapy from Eastern Washington University.
