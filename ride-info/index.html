So what is RAMROD?

RAMROD is a yearly ride organized by Redmond Cycling Club (RCC) in Redmond, WA where riders cover around 150 miles over about 10,000 feet of elevation in a single day! For more information see the FAQ on the RCC website.

How do I find out what is going on?

Follow us on social media. We will be regularly posting information and updates. And please feel free to reach out to us with questions on Facebook, Instagram, or Twitter!

How can I help?

Share She RAMROD with your friends! Get your bike tuned up and ready to ride. Share posts on social media with the #sheRAMROD hashtag and tag us in your posts.

If you want to help with planning or getting others involved, please reach out to us on social media. We are looking for ride leaders!
