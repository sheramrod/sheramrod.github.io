Welcome to the She RAMROD website!

We are aiming encourage, support, and empower women to Ride Around Mount Rainer in One Day (RAMROD) in 2020 so that 20% of riders are women! RAMROD is a yearly ride organized by Redmond Cycling Club in Redmond, WA where riders cover around 150 miles over about 10,000 feet of elevation in a single day!
